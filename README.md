### The Philosophy

"**You could leave life right now. Let that determine what you do and say and think**." > â€” Marcus Aurelius

Memento Mori is not about being morbid; it is about clarity. In our digital age, time often feels abstract. This project brings time back to the physical realm. By generating a precise grid representing your life (in weeks) or a specific journey (in days), it forces a confrontation with reality: Time is finite, and it is passing right now.

### The Ritual

This is not just a digital chart. The web page is merely the factory; the product is the paper on your wall.

Generate: Create your grid based on your age or a target goal.

Print: Export it to a clean, distraction-free PDF.

Fill: Physically fill in a square every day or week.

That simple act of darkening a square is a moment of reflection.

3. Click "Print / PDF" and save it.
